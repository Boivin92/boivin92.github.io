Touch of Life (Thaumaturgic Ritual ••)

The Touch of Life is an enlightening of the soul and the chakras of the body. It necessitates rigorous meditation and deep understanding of one's being to be unlocked by a student, but one born with this gift has been seen walking the South a hundred years ago - marked by the gift of life, it is rumored that his blood could even cure diseases.

Plants you touch become ripe for gathering or consumption within a minute or two. This ritual works on plants, fruits and vegetables even after harvesting. The larger the plant, the longer it takes for your touch to work. A minute or so for an apple, maybe an hour for an apple tree.

Using this ritual on a plant which should not be fruitful during this time (during a drought or winter, for example) takes a point of willpower and brings it back to a healthy state.

This power can never be turned off, but the user is not forced to pay the willpower cost when touching weak plants or fruits.
